So, how do I get to Pearl Hacks?

buses

Buses are the largest way we get girls to Pearl Hacks. This year we will be offering 2 interstate routes. Check the routes below to see if your school is a stop! If you are riding a bus to Pearl Hacks, please fill out this form.

car

If your school isn’t along a bus route, you can still be reimbursed for travel. Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more hackers in it (these girls do need to register, check in, and participate in the demo fair, to ensure that they are actually attending Pearl Hacks). Please see the chart to check how much of your gas we will cover. Please fill out the gas form if you need to apply for reimbursement. We have limited funds, which will be given on a first-come first-serve basis.

plane

Flights will be supplemented up to $300, on a first come first serve basis. Please do not purchase your flight until you have confirmation from our Director of Transportation, who will contact you after you submit your request through this form. Reimbursement will be a $300 Visa gift card that will be distributed at the hackathon to those who are approved, so the full cost of the flight will need to be covered until the weekend of the event.
We’ve filled all of our flight reimbursement spots for this year, please check out our bus routes to see if we pick up at a location near you!

No further reimbursements will be provided after February 1st.
