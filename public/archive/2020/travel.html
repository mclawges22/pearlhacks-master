Carpool

If your school isn’t on a bus route, you can still be reimbursed for travel. Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more participants in it (to ensure that they are actually attending Pearl Hacks, they all need to register, check in, and participate in the demo fair). Reimbursements will be issued on a first-come first-serve basis. Note that we are unable to reimburse costs incurred through Ubers or other ride-sharing apps.

Parking

Parking is available on the UNC campus for free on weekends and after 5pm on weekdays. We recommend parking at Cobb parking deck or Bell Tower Lot if Cobb Parking deck is not free. Use google maps to navigate to the parking deck and from the parking deck to the venue (Frank Porter Graham Student Union).

Other

Feel free to reach out to us with any other transportation-related questions at transportation@pearlhacks.com. We’re looking forward to having you at our hackathon!
