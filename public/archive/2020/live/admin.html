Mentors

Pearl Hacks is using a new system called HelpQ in order to organize our mentorship better. Use HelpQ to claim help tickets opened by participants who are looking for a mentor. For information about HelpQ, read our guide for mentors.

Mentors who pre-registered have accounts created for them, and will be contacted by email with their login information. If you have not received this information or want to be assigned a mentor role on a HelpQ account you've created using Google or GitHub authentication, contact Tasnia Sarwar or Jason Yu on Slack.
