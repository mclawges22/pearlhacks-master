Cars & Parking

Parking is available on the UNC campus for free on weekends and after 5pm on weekdays. We recommend parking at Cobb parking deck or Bell Tower Lot if Cobb Parking deck is not free.

Use google maps to navigate to the parking deck and from the parking deck to the venue (Frank Porter Graham Student Union).

Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more participants in it. To ensure that each passenger is actually attending Pearl Hacks, we ask that they all must register, check-in, and participate in the demo fair.
