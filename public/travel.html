Cars

If your school isn’t along a bus route, you can still be reimbursed for travel. Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more hackers in it (to ensure that they are actually attending Pearl Hacks, they all need to register, check in, and participate in the demo fair). Please check the chart to see how much of your gas we will cover. Please fill out the gas form if you need to apply for reimbursement. We have limited funds, which will be given on a first-come first-serve basis.

Other

If you decide to use any other means of transportation (e.g. train or a bus not on our route), please email transportation@pearlhacks.com about the possibility of reimbursement. Feel free to reach out to us regarding any other transportation related questions you may have too! We’re looking forward to having you at our hackathon!
